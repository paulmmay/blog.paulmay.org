Paul May

Paul May is a researcher, technologist, and interaction designer from Dublin, Ireland. He is currently working with Memorial Sloan Kettering; building data-driven products for oncologists, researchers, and patients. He is a graduate of NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program, which he attended as a Fulbright winner. He lives in Oxford, UK.

Work

  • Method Statement

    Method Statement is a growing collection of user experience research and design methods. Each method is free for you to use, adapt, and share.

  • News Faces

    News Faces is a bot that periodically tweets an image of a face, drawn from a public news source, and asks other Twitter users to confirm who the face belongs to. Face data is made publicly available.

  • From Over Here

    From Over Here is a data visualization of New York Times articles related to Ireland. The project is featured in the Gestalten book Visual Storytelling.

  • We Are Working on It

    We Are Working on It is a data visualization of scientific literature related to cancer research, accompanied by interviews with cancer researchers.

  • Abbey Theatre

    In 2010 I was part of the team who re-designed the website of Abbey Theatre, the National Theatre of Ireland.
