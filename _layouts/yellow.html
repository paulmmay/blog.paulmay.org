Blog

Paul May is a researcher and interaction designer from Dublin, Ireland. He is currently working with Memorial Sloan Kettering; helping them build data-driven products and services around the needs of doctors, researchers, and patients.

Paul has previously worked with Accenture, Front (now part of Monotype), and Frog for clients including Microsoft, BBC, O2, Abbey Theatre, Comhaltas, GE, Comcast, and TouchTunes.

Paul is a graduate of NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program, which he attended as a Fulbright winner.

When he's not working, Paul enjoys swimming, cycling, running, triathlon, photography, cooking, and coding. He lives in Oxford, England with his wife Cliona.
